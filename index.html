Web FFT benchmark

This page benchmarks many FFT implementations in your browser and plots the results in a graph. It assumes a modern browser that supports WASM SIMD. Only normal (complex) FFTs and power-of-2 sizes are compared for now. The benchmark is single core. All implementations work with 32-bit floats. In consistency with FFTw's benchmark, the rates (in FFTs per Î¼s) are multiplied by 5 N log2(N) for normalization.
